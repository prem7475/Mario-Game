# Level Design Patterns (10 reusable examples)

Use these patterns to author levels 1–100 as `LevelDefinition` ScriptableObjects.

1. **Warm-up Run**: flat ground + 1 enemy + coin line (teaches movement).
2. **Gap Trio**: three small gaps with safe landing pads + coin arcs to show jump timing.
3. **Hazard Arc**: spikes/fire on floor + coin arc over it to guide the safe path.
4. **Stomp Lane**: 2–3 walkers spaced so the player learns stomp chaining.
5. **Zig-Zag Platforms**: alternating left/right platforms increasing height (vertical control).
6. **Moving Bridge**: one moving platform over a pit, coins aligned to its travel path.
7. **Falling Gauntlet**: 3–5 falling platforms in a row; coins mark the intended rhythm.
8. **Split Path**: low route is safe but low coin yield; high route has hazards + strawberry.
9. **Flying + Walker Combo**: walker forces jump while a flyer punishes jumping too high.
10. **Checkpoint Challenge**: tight timing segment after checkpoint; reward coins on perfect route.
